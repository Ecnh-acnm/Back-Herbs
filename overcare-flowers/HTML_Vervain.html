Vervain

Verbana officinalis

Keywords

: Convert everyone's way of thinking.

Negative Indications

: Fixed principles and ideas, confidently right, rarely changing, highly strung, tense, and over-achieving.

Positive Outcomes

: Calm, wise, tolerant, relaxed, accepts the validity of other spiritual paths, and teaches best what we need to learn ourselves.

(Bach Center, 2007; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Vervain types are strong-willed with strong views and confident that they are always right. Their mind often races ahead and they take on too much work as they are inclined to tackle too many jobs at the same time. This strong will can force action beyond their physical strength.
  • They also take part in a distorted retelling of ‘truth’ and claim to have all answers. While they are sensitive to injustice and dedicated to causes they can alienate potential allies due to fanatical over-enthusiasm.
