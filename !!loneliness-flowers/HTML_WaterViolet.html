Water Violet

Hottonia palustris

Keywords

: Pride, Aloof.

Negative Indications

: Disdain or condescending towards others. Withdrawn and cold, cut-off, lonely, not friendly or forthcoming.

Positive Outcomes

: Comfortable connection with others; capable, understanding and empathetic. Talent in service of others, feels closer to them.

Clinical Applications and Uses


  • Calm, serene and dignified, but can appear proud, disdainful and condescending. Tendency to withdraw when tired or with too much external distraction; anti-social and cold.
  • Difficulty in opening up to others, can retreat into a calm facade. Water violet types are proud and protective of their delicate, highly spiritual nature.
  • Will experience longing to merge with others, but fearful of impact on their soft heart.

(Bach Center, 2007; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002)
