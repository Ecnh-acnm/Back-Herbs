Sundew

Drosera spatulata

Keywords

: Grounded. Living in the present.

Negative Indications

: Vague. Disconnected. Indecisive. Lack of focus. Daydreaming. Split. Procrastination. Dreamy. Drawn to drugs, Disassociation. Space cadet.

Positive Outcomes

: Attention to detail. Grounded. Focused. Living in the present. Return to the physical body. Interest in the world. Improve concentration.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Jan Wieneke, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • For those who have a broad sense of how something should be done but have difficulty with the fine detail. Helps to focus on the fine details of a task and to stop procrastinating. Vagueness and disconnectedness - which is a way of escaping from situations in life.
  • Often a response to shock or traumatic experience - disassociate from the present. Helps people return to the physical and the present. Good for fainting or recovery from anaesthetic or in a coma.
  • Originally for those under 28 years old, and Red Lily for those over 28 years old, but now interchangeable.
