Red Lily

Nelumbo nucifera

Keywords

: Grounded. Living in the present. Spiritual connection.

Negative Indications

: Vague. Disconnected. Indecisive. Lack of focus. Daydreaming. Split. Procrastination. Dreamy. Drawn to drugs. Disassociation. Grounding and connection to Higher Self.

Positive Outcomes

: Grounded, focused, living in the present, connection with life and Spirit. Centred. Balanced physical and emotional life.

(Ausflowers, 2023)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Helps to balance spiritual and earthly planes. Keeps people grounded and practical but still allows them to reach out to the spiritual planes. Vagueness, disconnection, split, indecisive, lack of focus, daydreaming. Tend to fantasise and live in the future, daydream, their minds are elsewhere.
  • On a physical level tend to be clumsy because they don't pay attention to what they are doing - may have a lot of accidents. May be drawn to drugs and hallucinogens. Red Lily can also be used by people who are suffering the aftereffects of taking drugs such as magic mushrooms and LSD. Also, can be used for people who are in a coma or are catatonic schizophrenics. Acts to open the Crown chakra and the higher chakras above the crown.
