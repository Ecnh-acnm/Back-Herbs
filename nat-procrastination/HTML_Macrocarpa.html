Macrocarpa

Eucalyptus macrocarpa

Keywords

: Exhaustion. Renewed enthusiasm and endurance.

Negative Indications

: Adrenal exhaustion. Drained. Jaded and worn-out. HP Axis dysfunction (too much stress).

Positive Outcomes

: Enthusiasm, inner strength, endurance.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Leif & Anita Stridvall, 1999)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Convalescent, tired, exhausted, burnt out, low immunity.
  • Works on the Adrenal glands - this type has burnt out their adrenal glands; lived on adrenalin (fight or flight). Good as a pick me up - the "Hornbeam" of ABFE.
  • Acts to raise the consciousness of the need to rest and releases extra energy. Gives a feeling of being more alive, energetic and in control. For times when the body is under great stress, e.g., marathon, endurance, labour, a big physical job with limited time. For lowered immunity and lots of sleep.

Combinations

  • With Black Eyed Susan - for burnout.
  • With Old Man Banksia and Swamp Banksia - for convalescence.
  • With Crowea - for worry debilitation.
