Jacaranda

Jacaranda mimosaefolia

Keywords

: Scattered. Always changing their minds.

Negative Indications

: Scattered, changeable, dithering. Rushing. All over the place.

Positive Outcomes

: Decisiveness, quick thinking, centred. Poised, clear-headed and quick-thinking. They are flexible and can judge a position appropriately and therefore make a decision.

(Ausflowers, 2023)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Jacaranda is a remedy for ditherers. Those who are always starting projects but rarely finish them. They have great difficulty in making decisions and constantly change their minds. They have a very scattered feeling about them and they tend to rush all over the place, here and there without achieving much at all.
  • The Jacaranda type take son many projects without focus and therefore the projects remain in various stages of incompletion. Yet they are enthusiastic, inspired and filled with excitement for the various projects, they just don't carry through. They are insecure about making the right decisions, which leads them to vacillate. At other times, they panic thinking they have made the wrong choice and so change their minds.

Illnesses:

Illnesses with changeable symptoms. They suffer from nervous rashes, hives, or pains that move about. They tend to be accident prone, and being unfocused they can be quite clumsy. Watch the video below for more information about Jacaranda.
