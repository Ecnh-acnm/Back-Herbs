Swamp Banksia

Banksia Robur

Keywords

: Temporary loss of drive and enthusiasm.

Negative Indications

: Low in vitality, temporary tiredness, frustration or setbacks for people who are normally dynamic and enthusiastic.

Positive Outcomes

: Enjoyment of life. Enthusiasm. Interest in life. Energy.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Tatters, 2021)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • De-energised, lacking vitality. Low in energy, disheartened, weary, frustrated.
  • Usually people who have:
    • Had a setback.
    • Haven't recovered from the flu or viral illness.
    • Had chronic fatigue, fibro-myalgia, or burnout.
  • Emotionally disappointed. Used to be vital and full of energy but now "I don't feel like myself." Tired and out of sorts.
