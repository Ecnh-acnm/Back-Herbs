Old Man Banksia

Banskia serrata

Keywords

: Sluggish and weary.

Negative Indications

: Sluggishness. Weary. Frustrated. Plethoric. Disheartened. Hypothyroidism.

Positive Outcomes

: Renews enthusiasm. Enjoyment and interest in life. The ability to cope with whatever life brings.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Sydneydawg, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • For solid, plethoric, heavy people who are often low in energy. OMB types are slow moving and have been sluggish for a long time. They are steady plodders.
  • They may have been disheartened by setbacks or just been weary for a long time. The sluggishness may be the result of low thyroid activity. OMB brings back the spark back into people's lives and gives them staying power.
This remedy continues to act in the body a long time after the remedy is finished. OMB types tend to be reliable and dependable. They are not such fast thinkers as Black-Eyed Susan types but they have more earthly natures and tend to operate from the emotional and physical planes rather than the mental.
They are practical, methodical, and patient. They don't rush. This type gives a lot of themselves to others - family, work, friends. For this reason, they can suffer from overload, overwork, and have difficulty saying no when they perceive others are relying on them. OMB helps them to realise their limits and learn to say now.
