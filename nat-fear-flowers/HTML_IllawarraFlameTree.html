Illawarra Flame Tree

Brachychiton acerifolius

Keywords

: Fear of responsibility and fear of rejection.

Negative Indications

: Afraid of responsibility. Overwhelmed by responsibility of their own life. Feeling of being left out, deeply hurt by perceived rejection.

Positive Outcomes

: Confidence, commitment, self-reliance, self-approval.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Bidgee, 2011).

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • For people who often feel rejected and/or left out.
  • They feel very deeply hurt when they perceive rejection - real or imaginary. These people feel that no one loves them if they are not included.
  • As a result, they often do things they would rather not do to avoid rejection.
  • These actions are a deep denial of the self, which can lead to a weakening of the thymus gland (the key to the immune system.
  • Flame tree types ignore their own potential.
