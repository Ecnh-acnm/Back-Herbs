Crowea

Crowea saligna

Keywords

: General worry and stress.

Negative Indications

: Fear or sense of things not being quite right. Continual worrying.

Positive Outcomes

: Peace and calm. Vitality. Balances and centres the individual.

(Ausflowers, 2023.)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Hurry, worry, and money - described as the evils of humanity. This is the worry remedy. For feeling not quite right. Used for emotional balance.
  • Calming, centring effect on the mind and body. Bring about a sense of well-being and aliveness. Will help to align the etheric and astral bodies with the physical body when these are out of balance. Targets the stomach and works with the muscles (strengthening action).

Illnesses

  • Regulates the amount of HCI in the stomach.
  • Stomach ulcers, butterflies in stomach.
  • Allergies as the result of undigested food.
  • Combines with Paw Paw for digestive imbalance.
  • Beneficial action on muscles and tendons - good for sporting injuries.
  • Moving from stage 1 to stage 2 labour - a good birthing remedy.
  • Assists the diaphragm - asthma, bronchospasms, intercostal chest muscles.
