Green Spider Orchid

Caladenia dilatata

Keywords

: Nightmares and phobias from past experiences. Spiritually blocked.

Negative Indications

: Fear of the sight of blood, nightmares.

Positive Outcomes

: Telepathic communication. Releases phobias and terrors. Ability to withhold information until timing is appropriate attunement.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Jean and Fred Hort, 2011.)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • This essence can work with telepathy between people and also improve communication between people. It helps people attune to and be more receptive to other people and also the plant and animal kingdoms.
  • It is excellent for those reaching out to new levels of awareness and for those teaching spiritual matters and understandings. It helps to fully comprehend the knowledge and then effectively impart it verbally and telepathically.
GSO helps an individual to keep information within. It creates a knowing about the appropriate time to share new information and reveal our spiritual experiences and projects with others. GSO can help release nightmares, terrors, and phobias arising from past lives. It is good in combination with the Grey Spider Flower for children with nightmares, etc.
It is also used for intense reactions to the sight of blood (hysteria, fainting, etc) which often stem from past life experiences.
