Bush Iris

Keywords

: Fear of death.

Positive Outcomes

: Aids spiritual awareness. Opens up to higher perception. Assists meditation. Balances pineal gland. Helps with acceptance of death. Enhances psychic perception.

(Ausflowers, 2023.)

  • Sexual excess.
  • Avarice.
  • For denial of spiritual (fear).

Illnesses

  • Balances the body clock.
  • SAD - helps balance the pineal gland and higher chakras.
