Hibbertia

Hibbertia pendunculata

Keywords

: Connect the heart with the intellect.

Negative Indications

: Fanatical about self-improvement. Strict and regimented with the self. Excessive self discipline. Superiority complex. Driven to aquire knowledge. Hypervigilance.

Positive Outcomes

: People are able to integrate ideas and information with experience and intuition which allows balance. Acceptance. Content with their own knowledge. Flexibility. Inner wisdow.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Casliber, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

Like most yellow flowers, Hibbertia deals with issues of intellect. It is for people who devour information and philosophies. These people have a great desire to learn, and they are very strict with themselves in the pursuit of knowledge to the point of being fanatical. The purpose behind the pursuit of knowledge is so they can feel superior to others, and is really just an intellectual exercise. They like to be in control and exhibit a lot of self denial and self-repression. Rigidity of the mind is often represented with stiffness of the body and can manifest in a lack of flexibility and hardened attitudes.

Conditions

  • Hardening diseases - atherosclerosis, fusing of the joints etc., rigidity, particularly in the bones.
  • This essence works on the parathyroid glands. Their secretion controls the metabolism of calcium and phosphorus in the body and therefore this essence can be used with any bone problems.
