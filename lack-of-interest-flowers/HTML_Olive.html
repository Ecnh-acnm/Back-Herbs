Olive

Olea europaea

Keywords

: Lack of energy. Tiredness, exhaustion due to overwork (physical and mental)

Negative Indications

: Mind wearied and exhausted with a lack of zest. Total fatigue of mind and body.

Positive Outcomes

: Convalescence, peace of mind, vitality, strength and interest.

Clinical Applications and Uses

:

  • Olive is exhausted in body and mind after a long period of strain through personal difficulties, intense study/work, long illness, nursing someone else for an extended time.
  • They may experience exhaustion to the point of tears and reserves of strength/energy runs out. They require a lot of sleep. Body functions are below par. Olive can be used as a support during intensive training for an event requiring endurance, and for recuperation.
  • Everything is an effort, tires easily, life lacks zest. No longer enjoys work/leisure. Energy has dissipated or been lost over a period of time for whatever reason.

(Bach Center, 2007; Chancellor, 1995; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002).
