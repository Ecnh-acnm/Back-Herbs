Honeysuckle

Lonicera caprifolium

Keywords

: Dwells on the past.

Negative Indications

: Over-attachment to past memories. Homesickness. Nostalgia.

Positive Outcomes

: Live in the present while seeing the past as a valuable experience providing lessons for today. Moving forward with no regrets. Ageing is seen as a natural process.

Clinical Applications and Uses

:

  • Honeysuckle believes the past is better than it was. They do not expect future happiness in comparison to the past and view the present and future with pessimism.
  • Someone else may feel obliged to make decisions for them, which can lead to co-dependency; Scleranthus types are left powerless to influence their own life-direction and quality. From this, they are rescued from the consequences of their mistakes. There will be constant hesitancy and indecisiveness, leading to persistent mental confusion.Relentless heartache for the homeland when beginning elsewhere. They wish to withdraw from cold reality.

"This is the remedy to remove from the mind the regrets and sorrows of the past, to counteract all influences, all wishes and desires of the past and to bring us back into the present.” (Dr Bach)

(Bach Center, 2007; Chancellor, 1995; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002).
