Clematis

Clematis vitalba

Keywords

: Dreaminess, lack of interest in the present.

Negative Indications

: Live in their own world, with no interest in reality. Yearn for better times. Prefer to be alone, avoid confrontation by withdrawing.

Positive Outcomes

: Lively interest and enjoyment in the world around them. Thoughts convert to action. Open to inspiration to fulfil creative potential. Realistic and down to earth with a purpose.

Clinical Applications and Uses

:

  • A Clematis has poor memory, poor attention to detail, have a lack of energy or appear absent-minded.
  • Their thoughts dwell in the future. They are imaginative, creative and fantasize about what may lie ahead – futuristic thoughts interfere with the enjoyment of present (bored, inattentive, day-dreamy, absent-minded).
  • They may escape to the future from current responsibilities (reading, movies, planning for future) – leaving no energy for day to day activities and may become reliant on others.
  • Part of rescue remedy – helps overcome faintness and emotional numbness.

(Bach Center, 2007; Chancellor, 1995; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002).
