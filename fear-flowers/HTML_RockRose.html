Rock Rose

Helianthemum nummularium

Keywords

: Extremely acute state of fear, terror, and panic.

Negative Indications

: Sense of frozen fear and helplessness, which under acute threat (such as natural disaster, sudden illness, etc).

Positive Indications

: Courage, presence of mind, calm and self-forgetting in emergencies.

Clinical Applications

: Clinical applications and uses of the rock rose include when the patient experiences:

  • The inability to cope with an acute threat: terror consumes the mind
  • Terror in anticipation of future events: exam, public performance
  • Impairment of senses by fear, body rigid with fear, or panicky nightmares
  • It is an important component of Rescue Remedy, good for instilling courage and steadfastness.
