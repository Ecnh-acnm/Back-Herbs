Cherry Plum

Prunus cerasifera

Keywords

: Fear of losing control.

Negative Indications

: Fear of losing control of behaviour or sanity, on the verge of a breakdown, deep despair.

Positive Indications

: Trusting, calm mind under stress, quiet courage, release from an obsession with control.

(Bach Center, 2007; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002)

Therapeutic Indications

: The clinical applications of the cherry plum include:

  • Loss of emotional control
  • May feel about to explode, afraid of giving way to violent impulses
  • Abnormally abusive and hysterical, exploding in sudden outbursts of rage
  • Desperation Acute remedy, part of Rescue Remedy
