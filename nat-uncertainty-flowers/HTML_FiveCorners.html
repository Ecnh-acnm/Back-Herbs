Five Corners

Styphelia triflora

Keywords

: Low self-esteem. Low self-worth.

Negative Indications

: Low self-esteem, dislike of Self. suppressed personality.

Positive Outcomes

: Love and acceptance of self, celebration of open beauty, joyousness.

(Ausflowers, 2023; John Tann, 2012)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Universal issues of self-esteem and self-acceptance.
  • Low self-esteem, particularly with the physical body, dislike of self, held in personality, drab clothing and colourlessness.
  • Loving the self, inside and out. Feeling loved, good about the self. Physical appearance - clothes, body, posture, facial expression (facing the world). Self sabotage - goals, relationships (feel unworthy to be loved). Five corners encourages self-love and therefore a belief in oneself and ability.
  • Needs to be used fairly long term to address sabotage and self-esteem and it may be an essence that comes up time and again for some people.

Illnesses

  • Acne, skin conditions - related to low self esteem.
