Sturt Desert Rose

Gossypium sturtianum

Keywords

: Guilt. Allows one to follow their own inner conviction and morality. Provides strength to be one's self.

Negative Indications

: Guilt, regret, and remorse. Low self-esteem. Easily led by others. Sexual guilt. Not true to oneself. Peer pressure. Feels paranoid that others are talking about them. Overly apologetic.

Positive Outcomes

: Courage, conviction, integrity. True to oneself, forgiveness of oneself. Restores self-esteem damaged by past actions.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Mark Marathon, 2015)

Clinical Applications and Uses

Clinical uses include for:

  • Guilt, regret over past actions. This essence helps people follow their own inner convictions and morality. It gives people the strength to be true to themselves. Restores self-worth. Resolves guilt on a sub-conscious level as well as a conscious level. In extreme cases, will blame themselves when things go wrong for others.
  • Designed to address guilt, including sexual guilt, this essence serves as a catalyst for resolving emotional triggers and promoting healing. By honouring your inner convictions and moral compass, it empowers you to take decisive action and follow through with integrity. Release feelings of regret and remorse as you realign with your authentic self, finding self acceptance.
Assists with personal integrity for those who lack self-esteem. Helps those experiencing guilt, who regret past actions/inactions, or who experience self-criticism and fault-finding. Beneficial for people who tend to be highly apologetic, wallow in inadequacy, or who judge themselves and are paranoid that others are judging them. Helps to release feelings of irrational self-blame when things go wrong for others.
