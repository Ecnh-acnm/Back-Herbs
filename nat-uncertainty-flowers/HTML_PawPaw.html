Paw Paw

Carica papaya

Keywords

: Overwhelmed.

Negative Indications

: Unable to resolve problems. Burdened by decision. Malabsorption of foods (like Crowea). Struggling with life decisions. Too much information.

Positive Outcomes

: Calmness, clarity, improved access to Higher Self for problem solving.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Ton Rulkens, 2012)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • When struggling for a major life decision, use this remedy. Strengthens the intuitive process and thereby helps to find solutions to our problems - activates as awareness of the Higher Self.
  • Paw Paw will instantly resolve the feelings of overwhelm surrounding decisions. Good for overexposure to new or difficult ideas or information. Brings about assimilation and integration of information and ideas.
  • Works as a digestive aid where the quality of food intake is diminished or where there is illness due to the malabsorption of food.
  • Improve access to Higher Self for problem solving, assimilation and integration of new ideas, calmness and clarity.

Dosage

  • Acute remedy so can be taken as a single dose - just before a seminar or workshop or before study.
  • One of the fastest acting remedies.
