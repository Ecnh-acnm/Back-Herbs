Monga Waratah

Telopea mongaensis

Keywords

: Disempowerment. Co-dependency. Find inner strength.

Negative Indications

: Neediness, co-dependency, inability to do things alone, disempowered, addictive personality.

Positive Outcomes

: Strengthens one's will. Reclaim one's spirit. Break dependency on behaviours, substances, or person. Self-empowerment.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Nicolas Smith, n.d.)

Clinical Applications and Uses

Clinical uses include helping a person find their inner strength which enables them to reach out.
Mongo Waratah addresses the negative conditions of:

  • Disempowerment.
  • Being overly needy.
  • Feeling not strong enough.
  • Feeling unable to do things alone.
  • Always needing the strength and support of others.
  • Feeling choked or stifled in situations or relationships.
  • Not being strong enough to leave.
This essence addresses co-dependency and acts to strengthens one's will, and is useful for treating addictions. This essence gives you the belief and sense that you can break out of dependency on a behaviour, substance, or person. It helps reclaim your spirit.
