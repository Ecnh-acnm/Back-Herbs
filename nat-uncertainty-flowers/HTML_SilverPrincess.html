Silver Princess

Eucalyptus caesia

Keywords

: Aimless, lack of direction.

Negative Indications

: Aimless, Despondent, lack of direction or inspiration. No clear goals.

Positive Outcomes

: Motivation. Direction. Life purpose.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Jarrah Tree, 2009)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • For people who are uncertain about their life plan or purpose.
  • This remedy is used for those who have come to a crossroad or a turning point and are not sure which step to take next. At these times, they have no clear goal or direction.
  • This essence can also help people to find a new direction once they have achieved a major goal. Silver Princess allows people to enjoy the journey towards their goal and once they have reached it, it gives them the motivation to pursue new goals in life. The effects of Silver Princess take effect within a short period of time, but it is a good idea to take this remedy longer than the customary 3 weeks.
