Pine

Pinus Sylvestri

Keywords

: Self-reproach and guilt.

Negative Indications

: Over-dutiful person who feels guilt-ridden over failure of obligation and is a self-reproachful perfectionist. Takes responsibility for the failings of others. Guilt and shame will destroy the possibility of joy.

Positive Outcomes

: Realistic levels of responsibility. Self-forgiveness; will accept and respect self without exaggerating negative judgement.

(Bach Center, 2007; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Regrets the past, so holds onto it in the hope it may change. Hopes to undo past imperfections by adopting a perfectionist attitude. Aims high, but falls short, reinforcing feelings of failure and guilt over letting themselves and others down.
  • Humble and apologetic.

One trace of condemnation against ourselves, or others, is a trace of condemnation against the Universal Creation of Love, and restricts us, limits our power to allow the Universal Love to flow through us to others.

(Dr Bach, n.d.)
