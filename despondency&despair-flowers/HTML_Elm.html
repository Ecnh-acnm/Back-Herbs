Elm

Ulmus procera

Keywords

: Suddenly overwhelmed by responsibilities and unable to complete life's tasks.

Negative Indications

: Feels inadequate to handle responsibility from taking on too many tasks. Can feel overwhelmed, anxious or depressed when overcommitted to external projects.

Positive Outcomes

: Self-assured competence, sense of responsibility to self; self-approval. Courage and poise when confronting life's tasks and ordeals.

(Bach Center, 2007; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Elm is indicated when responsibility once thrived on becomes crushing weight. For those who accumulate responsibility to provide secure structure and sense of worth. Self-imposed load becomes too heavy, and they will feel worthless and without direction.
  • Overwhelmed by the need to choose between desire to take on work to maintain self-worth and the need for relief from responsibly. Can have a tendency to drop the whole bundle and walk away.
  • When in balance, Elm types are very capable, the remedy also relieves the need for external validation.
