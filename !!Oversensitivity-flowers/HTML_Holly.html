Holly

Illex aquiflolium

Keywords

: Hatred, envy, jealousy, suspicion.

Negative Indications

: Aggressiveness (also passive aggressive), greed, extreme negativity, and insecurity. Belief that people are out to get them.

Positive Outcomes

: Generous; giving without demand for return. Compassionate, loving and loveable. Finds genuine pleasure in other people's success.

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Suppressed anger which turns to hate, envy, jealousy, misunderstanding, and revenge. Inability to love and feelings of generalised anger towards others and self (can be self-destructive/abusive).
  • Suffering, perhaps unconsciously, from insecurity; Holly types can be suspicious and aggressive. Hatred can be a defence mechanism to protect themselves from being hurt. Holly can help to soften the heart and address the unconscious shadow self.
  • For sensitive types, a long list of personal qualities that they like about themselves should be prepared before taking Holly.
