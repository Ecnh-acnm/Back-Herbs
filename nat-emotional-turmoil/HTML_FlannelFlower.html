Flannel Flower

Actinotus helianthi

Keywords

: Those who are uncomfortable with emotional or physical intimacy.

Negative Indications

: Dislike of being touched. Lack of sensitivity in males. Uncomfortable with intimacy.

Positive Outcomes

: Gentleness and sensitivity with touch. Trust. Openness. Expression of feelings. Joy in physical activity.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Casliber, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • For people who don't like physical contact - dislike being touched - particularly men. These people often crave closeness but feel like their space is being invaded. For lack of sensitivity in males - allows them to become more comfortable in expressing their gentler, softer side.
  • Increases sensuality, particularly in men - increases a joy in life in general as well as sex. Will enhance physical expression - increase physical activities and sport. Increases physical energy - feelings drive Qi in the body. If there is an increase in Qi there will be an increase in physical energy.
  • It helps to establish healthy boundaries with others.
