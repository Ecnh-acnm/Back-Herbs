Red Suva Frangipani

Plumeria rubra

Keywords

: Grief and sadness of relationship breakdown, or death of a loved one.

Negative Indications

: Initial grief, sadness, and upset of relationship issues/break-up. Emotional upheaval and turmoil. Death of a loved one.

Positive Outcomes

: Feeling calm and nurtured. Inner peace and strength to cope.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Casliber, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • This essence addresses the great emotional intensity, difficulty, and hardship that people go through when a relationship is ending, close to ending or when they are going through a rocky period.
  • Red Suva Frangipani helps to soothe the emotional rawness and intensity in relationships. It can also be used at any time where a relationship is going through a rough patch. Red Suva can also be taken for the great initial pain and sadness of the loss of a loved one. It can be added to Emergency Essence, which will help to deal with the shock and trauma, particularly if the death is sudden.
  • Red Suva is most potent when used in the middle of an emotional crisis, or when feeling greatly disturbed.
