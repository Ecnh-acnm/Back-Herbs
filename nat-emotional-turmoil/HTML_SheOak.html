She Oak

Casuarina glauca

Keywords

: Female wellbeing. Hormonal balance.

Negative Indications

: Female imbalance. Infertility based on non-physical reasons.

Positive Outcomes

: Nourishes the ovaries. Emotionally open to conceive. Balance and wellbeing for females.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Casliber, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

Inability to conceive. Related to the emotional factors inhibiting fertility. For women who are unable to fall pregnant for no apparent reason (20%). Clears any conscious or unconscious emotional blocks that may stop a woman from conceiving. e.g., feelings of inadequacy, lack of self confidence, worries about coping after the birth.
Some cases of infertility maybe be due to the dehydration of the uterus. Can help to clear mucus blockages from the Fallopian tubes and create healthy, cervical mucous. Helps to regulate the production of female hormones, particular where the ovaries are functioning spasmodically resulting in an irregular menstrual cycle. The hydrating action of She Oak will maintain moist, vaginal secretions so that sex does not become painful.
