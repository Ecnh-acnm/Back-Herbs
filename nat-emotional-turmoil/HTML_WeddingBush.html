Wedding Bush

Ricinocarpus pinifolius

Keywords

: Commitment to relationships or goals.

Negative Indications

: Difficulty with commitment.

Positive Outcomes

: Commitment to relationships. Commitment to goals. Dedication to life purpose.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Casliber, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • For difficulty making commitments, whether to relationships, employment, the family, or to personal goals. Excellent to take at the beginning of a partnership or any form of bonding. Helps to provide dedication to a task or relationship to one's life purpose. Helpful for recommitment to current relationship.
  • Also used to stop one from jumping from relationship-to-relationship i.e., increases commitment to current relationship if in one or to oneself. Casanova complex. Good for those who have taken on activities or regimens which require discipline and commitment, e.g., athletes, persons who want to abstain from something, lose weight, or become healthier.
