Wisteria

Wisteria sinensis

Keywords

: Uncomfortable with sexuality.

Negative Indications

: Uncomfortable with sex. Closed sexually. Mostly a female remedy, but also works with "Macho Males" to access their softer side.

Positive Outcomes

: Sexual enjoyment. Enhanced sensuality. Sexual openness. Gentleness.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Casliber, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Remedy relates predominately to women, but can be used by men who are incapable of accessing their emotional side - macho males. For women who feel generally uncomfortable and uptight about their sexuality. For those who feel tense about sex and are unable to relax and enjoy it. Those who are afraid of intimacy or even of expressing their sensual sides.
  • These feelings may be directly attributable to previous sexual abuse or assault. Use where there are any cervical cancers, STI's, vaginal discharges - mind/body connection e.g., they may feel unable to have sex because of an STI. Using with Billy Goat Plum if feeling dirty. For me, it allows them to feel and express their gentler and softer sides.
