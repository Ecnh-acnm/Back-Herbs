Billy Goat Plum

Planchonia careya

Keywords

: Shame.

Negative Indications

: Inability to enjoy sex. Sense of shame, discomfort, and self-loathing. embarrassment regarding physical aspects such acne, eczema, etc.

Positive Outcomes

: Sexual pleasure and enjoyment. Acceptance of oneself. Open mindedness. Look deeper to see true beauty. Foster a positive relationship with their body.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Casliber, 2006)

Clinical Applications and Uses

For feelings of self-disgust and loathing, especially when associated with sexual organs and/or the sex act. Note:

  • Unclean- this is the trigger word for this remedy.
  • Shame - sense of being exposed.
Also for feelings of internalised loathing that can follow from sexual violation. and for attitudes of dirtiness around sex, perspiration, and periods. Where there is any form of physical loathing.

Conditions

Use with VD, genital warts, and herpes. Skin conditions - good applied topically to acne.

Combinations

For a holistic approach use with Five Corners, especially when feelings of unattractiveness are stopping the thought of an intimate relationship.
