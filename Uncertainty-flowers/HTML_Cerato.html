Cerato

Ceratostigma willmottianum

Keywords

: Seeks advice and confirmation from others.

Negative Indications

: Lack of self-belief, with negative self judgement. Lacking confidence in their own judgement and self-doubt; a search for external authority.

Positive Indications

: Promotes certainty and faith in one's own intuition. Contact with inner guidance and creation of a life that reflects one's true, positive personality.

Clinical Applications and Uses

:

  • A Cerato type will question judgement; having made a decision, they will doubt that it is the right one. They seek guidance, reassurance or the confirmation of others.
  • They will misguidedly follow someone else’s advice rather than trust themselves, but will seek advice they wish to hear. Thus, they will be stuck in the same patterns, by seeking permission to stay the same. Ceratos are always asking advice, and are talkative so as to sap the vitality of others. When ill, they will try a succession of treatments recommended by others.
  • An actor, this means they will have no identity outside of their role.

(Bach Center, 2007; Chancellor, 1995; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002).
