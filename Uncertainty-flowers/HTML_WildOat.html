Wild Oat

Bromus ramosus

Keywords

: Uncertainty as to the correct path in life.

Negative Indications

: Crossroads reached, undecided, uncertain and unable to choose one of many paths. Despondent, frustrated and dissatisfied, life passing them by.

Positive Outcomes

: Knowledge of true vocation or purpose in life: definite character, talents, and ambitions.Lives a life with usefulness and happiness.

Clinical Applications and Uses

:

  • These people will be drifters and feel apathetic, but with Wild Oat, they can prioritise and redefine their future direction.
  • Those desiring a change of direction, experiencing a mid-life crisis or vocational crisis are indicated and can finally hear their life calling. They will be a 'jack of all trades and a master of them too'.
  • Wild Oat acts as a vocational guide for the soul.

(Bach Center, 2007; Chancellor, 1995; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002).
