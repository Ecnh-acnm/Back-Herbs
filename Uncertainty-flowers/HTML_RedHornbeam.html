Native

Hornbeam

Carpinus betulus

Keywords

: Monday morning feeling.

Negative Indications

: Weariness, mental fatigue. Overwork or staleness and lack of vitality in life; lack of enthusiasm and may procrastinate.

Positive Outcomes

: Certainty of strength and ability to face the day's work. Lively mind, vitality, freshness and spontaneity restored; life is enjoyable. Work faced with energy, clear head and properly balanced with play.

Clinical Applications and Uses

:

  • Tiredness, weariness, mental exhaustion. Useful during convalescing.
  • 'Mondayitis' all day, every day. What needs to be done seems unsurmountable and unavoidable; everything is a chore. Energy is wasted on concern over whether or not jobs will get done; can't cope, with no strength or energy.
  • Hornbeam can help to develop fresh patterns with healthy rhythms, restores choice, energy and strength to change.

(Bach Center, 2007; Chancellor, 1995; Scheffer, 1988; Wells, 2002).
