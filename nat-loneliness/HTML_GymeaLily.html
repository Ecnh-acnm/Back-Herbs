Gymea Lily

Doryanthes excelsa

Keywords

: Excessive pride and arrogance.

Negative Indications

: Arrogant, attention-seeking, craves status and glamour, dominating personality.

Positive Outcomes

:Humility, allowing others to express themselves and contribute, awareness, appreciation and taking note of others and allowing one to reach great heights.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Adam J.W.C, 2007)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Gymea Lily allows a person to draw from its powerful energy and attain the strength to be who they are and to do what they have to do. It enables people to follow their passion, to do what makes their heart sing, ultimately what fulfils their highest destiny.
  • This essence helps you to stay strong and focused on doing what you know you have to do with such single-minded purpose that you are no worried about what others think. Being successful can be a threat to some people, often because it focuses attention on what they are doing with their lives or areas that they are unhappy with.
  • Gymea Lily can also be used for people whose personalities are very extroverted but also dominating and charismatic and used to getting their own way. Gymea Lily tempers this tendency to always take charge. It helps them be a bit more aware of other people and consider where it is at all necessary for them to take charge. Gymea Lily also addressed attention seeking behaviour and the constant need for status and glamour.

Illnesses

  • Has an action on the spine, as well as working on the bones and ligaments.
