Pink Mulla Mulla

Ptilotus exaltatus

Keywords

: Heals deep spiritual wounds. Pushes people away to prevent hurt.

Negative Indications

: Deep spiritual wounds on the psyche. Guarded, prickly persona to prevent being hurt. Keeps people at a distance. Fear of being hurt.

Positive Outcomes

: Deep spiritual healing. Trusting and opening up. Clears sabotage.

(Ausflowers, 2023)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Pink Mulla Mulla is for those who have suffered a deep, ancient, spiritual wound on the psyche. This has led to an old scar being left on their psyche.
  • Consequently, we will put into sabotages into place at a very deep level in the body, well hidden from our consciousness and psyche. On an emotional level, Pink Mulla Mulla is for those who put out prickles to keep others away. They tend to be quite isolated, and are loners by choice. They often feel wronged or hurt, or they believe have been treated unjustly in the past which has affected them deeply. This impinges on their attitude to those around them, and can make them suspicious of people and people's motives.
  • Pink Mulla Mulla allows them to open up, trust, and interact with people more freely. This is quite a fast acting remedy assisting people to overcome their resistances very quickly. On a physical level, it helps to overcome stiffness which is a direct consequence of isolation and any spiritual wounds on the psyche.

Illnesses

Stiffness.
