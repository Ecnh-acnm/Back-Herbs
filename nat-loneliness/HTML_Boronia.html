Boronia

Boronia ledifolia

Keywords

: Obsessive thoughts.

Negative Indications

: Obsessive thoughts, fullness in head, insomnia, difficulty concentrating. Pining, broken-hearted.

Positive Outcomes

: Clarity, serenity, creative visualisation.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Doug Beckers, 2009)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Letting go of the past. For obsessive thinking - thoughts that keep going over and over in the mind. Infatuation, falling in love - addicted to love, can't be without love.
  • Good for eliminating habits/habitual thoughts/habitual emotional states. Mental chatter.

Positive Impacts

: Clarity of thought and mind, serenity, mental calmness. Good for creative visualisation - helping to focus positively. Good for goal setting and achieving. Mental chatter - gives the ability to scan but not focus. Breaks vicious cycles of thought patterns creating repetitive behaviour.

Illnesses

: Madness, schizophrenia, psychosis, obsessive/compulsive behaviour, anti-life habits.
