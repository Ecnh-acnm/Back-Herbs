Fringed Violet

Thysanotus tuberosus

Keywords

: Energetic protection.

Negative Indications

: Lack of psychic protection. Sensitive to other energies. Drained by other people. Poor recuperation after shock and trauma. Distress.

Positive Outcomes

: Removal of effects of recent or old trauma. Reintegration of physical and etheric bodies. Psychic protection.

(Ausflowers, 2023; John Tann, 2014)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • Damage to a person's aura - shock (can throw off internal balance), drugs, anaesthetic, alcohol, power lines. Useful post-surgery, slow recovery, or for people who have never recovered following a shock or bad news - enables them to release the long-held trauma.
  • Shock - there can be a delayed reaction, or it can be stored in the tissues for weeks or even years. People may develop a skin rash or eczema some time later. Unresolved shock can manifest in nervous system conditions such as nervous breakdown.
  • Excellent to ward off emotional vampires or protect people who are open to being drained by others - e.g., massage therapists. Good for age regression work, or re-birthing and birth trauma. Helps to set boundaries in personal growth work.

Combinations

Sexual abuse - with Flannel Flower for men, and with Wisteria for women.
