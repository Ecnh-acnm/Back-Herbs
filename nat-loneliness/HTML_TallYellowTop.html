Tall Yellow Top

Senecio magnificus

Keywords

: Alienation. No sense of belonging.

Negative Indications

: Alienation. Isolation. Loneliness. No feeling of connection or belonging.

Positive Outcomes

: Sense of belonging. Acceptance of self and others. Knowing that you are "home." Ability to reach out to others.

(Ausflowers, 2023; Mark Marathon, 2012)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • This essence is for loneliness and isolation. It addresses alienation where there is a lack of connection with no sense of belonging to any community - family, workplace, or country. It is also for lack of love - Tall Yellow Top helps to reconnect the head and heart.
  • Often, Tall Yellow Top types have lived in this state for a long time. Consequently, the essence needs to be taken for 6 to 8 weeks at a time. The benefits will be gradual. This essence works well for people who feel that they are strangers in a strange land and that they don't belong. They feel like they have been sent to the wrong place, where they just don't fit in.
