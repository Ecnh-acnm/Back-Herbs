Waratah

Telopea speciosissima

Keywords

: "Dark night of the Soul." Gives strength and courage.

Negative Indications

: "Dark night of the soul." Despair, hopelessness, inability to respond in a crisis.

Positive Outcomes

: Gives courage and faith to keep going, enables people to call on their inner strength brings awareness and understanding.

(Ausflowers, 2023)

Clinical Applications and Uses

  • For the black night of the soul - feelings of utter despair. Suicidal tendencies and depression. Times of war or tragedy.
  • Gives courage the ability to cope with their crises. This remedy will bring forth any survival skills we have learnt before and amplify them. Very quick acting - initial benefits are immediate, and the full effects are achieved within a few days.

Illnesses

Used for Ventricular failure, and mitral valve insufficiency in particular, but heart imbalances in general. used effectively for glaucoma - halts the condition and can also reverse it. Used in hospitals to overcome transplant rejection.

Combinations

Used in EMERGENCY ESSENCE.
