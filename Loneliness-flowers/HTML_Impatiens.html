Impatiens

Impatiens glandulifera

Keywords

: Impatience

Negative Indications

: Irritability and frustration, especially under stress. Extreme mental tension.

Positive Outcomes

: Capable, decisive, spontaneous, intuitive and clever. Less hasty in thought and action, will cope calmly and diplomatically with irritating problems. Gentle, understanding, tolerant, and sympathetic to others.

Clinical Applications and Uses


  • Impatiens types are independent; thinking, acting and speaking quickly. They are capable and efficient with a desire for promptness, which can cause irritation and frustration with slow co-workers. Can lash out with words or deeds; suggestions of meditation may be met with resistance or disdain.
  • They hate wasting time, and will hurry conversations, fidget, feel on edge and eager to get to the next thing. Can have temper flare-ups. The Impatiens remedy can help to restore balance and heighten awareness of reactionary patterns to allow these people to respond with diplomacy.
  • Active and nervous, where hastiness can lead to accidents. Will run around in circles, often accomplishing less than a slower person.
